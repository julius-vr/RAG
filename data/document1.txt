Innovations in Renewable Energy
---------------------------------
The renewable energy sector has seen tremendous innovation over the past decade, driven by the urgent need to address climate change. Recent advances in solar photovoltaic technology have led to higher conversion efficiencies and lower costs, making solar power a viable alternative to fossil fuels on a large scale. Wind energy has also evolved, with taller turbines and smarter grid integration systems enabling more consistent power output. In addition, breakthroughs in energy storage and smart grid management are paving the way for a more resilient and sustainable energy infrastructure.

Source: Global Energy Journal
